Use Cases & Recommendations

🏢 Choose Excel When:

• Advanced financial modeling and complex data analysis
• Working with massive datasets (millions of rows)
• Need sophisticated statistical functions
• Require offline access and desktop performance
• Industry-specific requirements (finance, engineering)

👥 Choose Google Sheets When:

• Real-time team collaboration is essential
• Budget constraints (free for personal use)
• Simple to moderate data analysis needs
• Remote teams requiring cloud accessibility
• Integration with Google Workspace ecosystem

📊 Choose Airtable When:

• Project management and workflow automation
• Need database functionality with spreadsheet ease
• Content management and CRM applications
• Visual data organization (Kanban, Calendar views)
• Team coordination with structured data relationships

💡 Quick Decision Guide

Data Analyst/Financial Professional: Choose Excel
Small Team/Startup: Choose Google Sheets
Project Manager/Operations: Choose Airtable
